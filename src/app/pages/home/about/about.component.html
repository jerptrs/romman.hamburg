Romman

We are more than a restaurant. We are more than culture, more than art. Romman came to be in summer 2020, found by various people who came together for various reasons. Friends, old and new met here at Romman. We came and we stayed. Romman is an idea- our idea. And yours. It is a special place that brought us together, it is a territory where everyone can just be. As this place grows everyday, so do we: in our minds, souls and friendship. Romman is not a restaurant. It is an idea. An idea that keeps evolving. Be part of it. We invite you to visit us - or if you'd like, we also deliver in Eppendorf and Winterhude. Just hit your order and your location on Whatsapp.
